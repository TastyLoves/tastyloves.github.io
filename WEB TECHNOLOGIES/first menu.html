Creamy Tomato Pasta



This creamy tomato pasta dish is easy to make and uses common ingredients. The velvety tomato sauce will wow you!

Making a creamy tomato pasta sauce from scratch with canned tomatoes is straightforward! The best thing is that it can be ready by the time your spaghetti is done. Ideal for busy weeknights.

I use a can of tomato sauce and add some tomato paste to amp up the taste of the sauce. Garlic, Italian spice, butter, and cream round out the flavour profile. And freshly grated parmesan cheese tops everything off!

Instructions

  1. Boil a big pot of salted water for your pasta and cook it al dente according to package directions.

  2. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a pan over medium-high heat (after the pasta has begun to cook). Once it has melted, add the garlic, onion and saut√© for approximately a minute, stirring frequently.

  3. Once the onion has softened, add the chicken and fry (be careful not to burn them or overcrowd the pan).

  4. Combine the tomato paste, tomato sauce, cream, and Italian seasoning in a mixing bowl. Stir until completely smooth. Allow the sauce to simmer for 5 minutes, or until it has thickened to your preference (it should be very gently bubbling, so you may need to turn down the heat).

  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste (I'm a big fan of both). If the sauce is too acidic/tangy for your taste, you can add a pinch of sugar if you want to.

  6. Toss the pasta with the sauce once it has been drained (add a splash of hot pasta water if the sauce gets too thick). Serve immediately with a generous sprinkle of parmesan cheese on top.
